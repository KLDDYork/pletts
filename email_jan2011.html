This month : What's the true cost of recruiting?

In this age of austerity we're all trying to find ways of trimming costs and making our budgets work harder. Do it yourself recruitment is often perceived to be one way to save professional recruitment agency fees. But have you considered the real cost of the DIY approach?

DIY recruitment can often be a hit and miss solution which doesn't guarantee you'll always find the right candidate for the job. When you consider all that goes into the process, you may also be surprised just how much it can cost. For example, you need to account for the following costs:

  • online and press advertising
  • advert design, proofing and production
  • the time taken to filter applications for interview
  • the time cost of interviewing candidates
  • chasing up referees
  • providing feedback to candidates

It's often the case that these factors can be more than double the cost of using a recruitment agency that handles much of the process on your behalf.
